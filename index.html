SIXTEEN Lazy Days of Chocolate

Do you crave chocolate? Do you want to create and eat something chocolaty and amazing, but don't want to work too hard? You are in the right place. Pick any chocolate from the box. Each chocolate will take you to a different, delicious, easy chocolate recipe somewhere on the web. You never know what you're going to get. And if you don't like the chocolate you picked, you can always take a bite and put it back.
